Green Mobility

This website section addresses the environmental consequences of using non-renewable fuels in transportation, detailing the benefits of switching to renewable energy sources and electric or hybrid vehicles. It offers practical steps for individuals to adopt more sustainable transportation methods, such as carpooling and using public transit, encouraging a shift towards a greener future.
