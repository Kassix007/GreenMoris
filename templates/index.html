Meet Menmen

Meet Menmen, a passionate coder with a deep love for the ocean. One sunny afternoon, while swimming at Flic en Flac beach, Menmen was heartbroken to see the beautiful shoreline polluted by litter. On his way back, he decided to munch on some gato piment, which was packed in plastic bags causing climate pollution. Menmen also realized that the fuel used to run his car was non-renewable too. He reached back home with a sad smile. Determined to make a difference, Menmen decided to develop a website.
